Q&A

What is &udm=14?

&udm=14 is a URL parameter you can add to a Google search result URL that removes all the new AI and ad popups.

Why should I use &udm=14?

You can use this parameter to tell Google to ads & remove AI results from your search. This will prevent ads & generative AI from appearing in your search results.

Can I do this myself?

Yes, you can do this yourself by typing something along the lines of

https://google.com/search?q=YOUR SEARCH HERE&udm=14

into your search bar.
